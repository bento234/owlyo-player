Privacy Policy for Owlyo Podcast Player

Last updated: 10.05.2026

Deutsche Version

1. Data Controller

The controller responsible for data processing under applicable data protection laws (including GDPR where applicable) is:

  • Name: Benjamin Jürgens
  • Address: P.O. box pending
  • Email: owlyo-support@bendreality.de

2. Overview

Owlyo Podcast Player is an ad-free podcast player with a parent area and PIN protection.

  • no account registration
  • no user accounts
  • no analytics or tracking tools
  • no active sharing of personal data by the app itself

3. Data Processed by the App

3.1 Local Device Data (not transmitted to us)

The app stores technically necessary information locally on the device, for example:

  • approved podcasts and episodes
  • playback progress
  • settings (e.g. parent PIN as hash only, never plain text)

This data stays on the device and is not sent to us.

3.2 Network Requests Required for Functionality

To search for and play podcasts, the app connects to external services:

  • Podcast Index API (search and metadata)
  • RSS feed servers of selected podcasts
  • audio servers of podcast providers (streaming)

During these connections, technically necessary connection data (e.g. IP address, timestamp, user agent) is processed by the respective servers. We have no direct control over that processing.

4. No Ads, No Tracking

  • no advertising
  • no third-party trackers
  • no crash analytics
  • no profiling

5. Legal Basis

Where GDPR applies, processing is based in particular on Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (provision of app functionality) and Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in secure and stable operation). If consent is required, Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR applies.

6. Storage Duration

Locally stored app data remains until either:

  • it is deleted inside the app, or
  • the app is uninstalled.

7. Recipients and International Transfers

We do not actively transmit personal data to third parties ourselves. Through the use of external podcast and API services, processing may occur outside the EU/EEA. In such cases, the privacy policies of the respective providers apply.

8. Data Subject Rights

Under GDPR (where applicable), data subjects have rights including access, rectification, erasure, restriction, data portability, objection, and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

Requests can be sent to the contact email listed above.

9. Children and Youth Protection

The app is functionally designed for parents to curate podcast content for children. The parent area is PIN-protected.

10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy due to legal, technical, or content changes.
